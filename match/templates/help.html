Help

Instructions

The left hand-side of the screen shows the addreses of a job centre, while the right hand-side shows possible matches from AddressBase. If you find an address that matches, just click it. If none match, click "No match", and if you're not sure, click "Not sure".

You only need to match the addresses - the name of the job centre is not important. The more matches you make and the more correct they are, the more you score.

Background

The goal is to find the definitive address of each job centre.

The address shown on the left hand-side is from a list of job centres. The addresses on the right are drawn from AddressBase data containing a Unique Property Reference Number (UPRN). We want to find the UPRN of each job centre, by matching the job centre text address on the left to the correct UPRN-associated address on the right.

Matching addresses is hard. Automated matching only takes us so far, and typically misses the most complex addresses. This tool takes the crowd-sourcing approach: participants are asked to match addresses manually from a list of possible candidates.

Your score will reflect the number of matches you've made, as well as how well your guesses match other people's. Enough matches will allow us to establish with a good degree of certainty the correct answer.
